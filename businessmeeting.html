District 56 Monthly Business Meeting


On the third Wednesday of each month (at 6:00 PM PST), District 56 hosts its monthly business meeting. It is an in person meeting, as well as, Zoom.
The location of the in person meeting is Grace Lutheran Church, 1120 Walker St., Port Townsend, WA 98368
These meetings are open to all AA members, regardless if they are involved in general service within District 56. If you'd like to join our next business meeting, please use the link below. The Zoom login information is also available on our meeting schedule.
